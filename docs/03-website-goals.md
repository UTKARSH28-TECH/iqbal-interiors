\# WEBSITE GOALS \& USER EXPERIENCE



\# Purpose



This document defines the vision, user experience, and success criteria for the Iqbal Interiors website.



Every design and development decision should support these goals.



\---



\# Primary Goal



Create a premium digital showroom that showcases Iqbal Interiors' completed work and product range, encouraging visitors to contact the business through WhatsApp, phone, or a showroom visit.



The website should replace the need to send dozens of individual photos to customers.



\---



\# Business Goals



The website should:



\* Build trust with first-time visitors.

\* Showcase the company's craftsmanship.

\* Display the complete range of services.

\* Present premium products professionally.

\* Increase WhatsApp enquiries.

\* Increase phone calls.

\* Increase showroom visits.

\* Strengthen the Iqbal Interiors brand.



\---



\# User Goals



Visitors should be able to:



\* Quickly understand what Iqbal Interiors offers.

\* Browse interior designs by category.

\* Explore completed projects.

\* View premium false ceiling designs.

\* Discover lighting and jhumar collections.

\* Explore sanitary and hardware products.

\* Contact the business in one click.



\---



\# User Journey



A successful visitor journey should look like this:



Visitor lands on homepage



↓



Feels impressed by the hero section



↓



Explores services



↓



Browses gallery categories



↓



Views completed work



↓



Shares gallery with family



↓



Contacts Iqbal Interiors



↓



Visits showroom or requests a quotation



\---



\# Website Personality



The website should feel:



\* Luxury

\* Elegant

\* Modern

\* Minimal

\* Professional

\* Trustworthy

\* Warm

\* Spacious



Avoid visual clutter.



\---



\# First Impression



Within the first five seconds, visitors should understand:



\* This is a professional interior design company.

\* The company has completed real projects.

\* The quality of work is high.

\* Contacting the company is easy.



\---



\# User Experience Principles



Every page should be:



\* Easy to navigate

\* Fast loading

\* Image focused

\* Mobile friendly

\* Accessible

\* Visually consistent



The design should encourage exploration without overwhelming the visitor.



\---



\# Mobile Experience



Most users will visit from WhatsApp links.



Therefore:



\* Mobile experience is the highest priority.

\* Buttons should be large and touch-friendly.

\* Images should remain sharp.

\* Navigation should be simple.

\* Contact actions should always be easy to access.



\---



\# Conversion Strategy



Each page should gently encourage visitors to:



\* Explore another gallery.

\* View additional projects.

\* Contact the business.

\* Visit the showroom.



Calls to action should feel natural rather than aggressive.



\---



\# Gallery Experience



The gallery is the most valuable part of the website.



It should:



\* Load quickly.

\* Be easy to browse.

\* Support category pages.

\* Support direct sharing.

\* Scale easily as new images are added.



Adding new images should require minimal developer effort.



\---



\# Success Metrics



The website is successful if visitors:



\* Spend time exploring galleries.

\* Share gallery links.

\* Contact the business through WhatsApp.

\* Call the business.

\* Visit the showroom.



\---



\# Long-Term Vision



The website should grow with the business.



Future additions should be possible without redesigning the entire website.



Potential future features include:



\* Video gallery

\* Customer reviews

\* Blog

\* Project case studies

\* Cost estimator

\* Appointment booking

\* Multi-language support



The architecture should be designed with these future enhancements in mind.

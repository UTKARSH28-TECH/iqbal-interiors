\---



\## Project Vision



Develop a premium, modern, luxury interior design and showroom website that builds trust immediately and converts visitors into WhatsApp enquiries and showroom visits.



The website should look comparable to professionally designed interior studio websites rather than generic template websites.



The design philosophy should emphasize:



\- Luxury

\- Simplicity

\- Spacious layouts

\- Premium typography

\- High-quality photography

\- Smooth animations

\- Mobile-first responsive design

\- Fast loading performance



\---



\## Website Objectives



1\. Showcase completed interior projects.

2\. Showcase product categories available in the showroom.

3\. Increase WhatsApp enquiries.

4\. Increase physical showroom visits.

5\. Improve local brand credibility.

6\. Rank well on Google.

7\. Make sharing gallery pages easy.

8\. Provide a professional online portfolio.

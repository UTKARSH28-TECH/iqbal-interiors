\# CONTENT STRATEGY



\# Purpose



This document defines how content should be presented throughout the Iqbal Interiors website.



The objective is to communicate professionalism, quality craftsmanship, and trust while keeping the website clean, modern, and visually driven.



Content should always support the photography rather than compete with it.



\---



\# Content Philosophy



The website should prioritize:



\* Large project photography

\* Minimal but meaningful text

\* Clear section hierarchy

\* Easy readability

\* Strong calls to action



Avoid unnecessary paragraphs.



Every section should have a purpose.



\---



\# Writing Tone



The tone should be:



\* Professional

\* Friendly

\* Confident

\* Premium

\* Trustworthy

\* Simple



Avoid exaggerated marketing language.



Instead of saying:



"We are the best."



Prefer:



"We create thoughtfully designed interior spaces tailored to your lifestyle."



\---



\# Homepage Content Flow



The homepage should tell a complete story.



Visitors should understand:



1\. Who the company is.

2\. What services it provides.

3\. Why they should trust the company.

4\. What work has been completed.

5\. How to contact the business.



\---



\# Hero Content



The hero section should communicate:



\* Premium interior design

\* Beautiful craftsmanship

\* Complete interior solutions



The headline should be short and memorable.



Example style:



"Design Spaces You'll Love"



Supporting text should explain the value in one or two concise sentences.



\---



\# Services Content



Each service should contain:



\* Service title

\* One-line description

\* Representative image



Descriptions should focus on customer benefits rather than technical details.



\---



\# Gallery Content



The gallery is the website's strongest marketing asset.



Each gallery page should include:



\* Category title

\* Short introduction

\* High-quality image grid

\* WhatsApp CTA

\* Related categories



Text should remain secondary to the images.



\---



\# Featured Projects



Each featured project should include:



\* Project name

\* Short summary

\* Cover image



Future support:



\* Project location

\* Completion year

\* Materials used

\* Design highlights



\---



\# About Section



The About section should tell the company's story.



Include:



\* Company background

\* Years of experience

\* Commitment to quality

\* Customer-first approach



Focus on building trust.



\---



\# Owner Section



Introduce the owner with:



\* Professional photograph

\* Brief biography

\* Experience

\* Personal philosophy



This section helps create a stronger connection with visitors.



\---



\# Contact Content



Keep the contact section simple.



Display:



\* Phone number

\* WhatsApp

\* Address

\* Google Maps

\* Business hours



Calls to action should be clear and visible.



\---



\# Calls to Action



Primary CTAs:



\* Explore Gallery

\* View Collection

\* WhatsApp Now

\* Call Now

\* Visit Showroom



Buttons should encourage action without feeling aggressive.



\---



\# SEO Content



Every page should include:



\* Unique page title

\* Meta description

\* Descriptive headings

\* Image alt text



Content should be written naturally for both users and search engines.



\---



\# Future Content Expansion



The content structure should support:



\* Blog articles

\* Interior design tips

\* Project case studies

\* Customer testimonials

\* Frequently Asked Questions

\* Video showcases



without requiring major redesign.



\---



\# Final Principle



Photography tells the story.



Text supports the story.



The website should inspire visitors through visuals first and information second.
